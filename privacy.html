Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

Vanderven Systems (“we,” “us,” “our”) provides websites, AI automation, and marketing for real estate, property management, professional services, and trades across the Okanagan. This policy explains what personal information we collect through vandervensystems.com and our client work, how we use it, and the choices you have. We handle personal information in accordance with British Columbia’s Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and, where applicable, the federal Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you fill out a contact form, book a free audit, request a quote, or communicate with us by email. This may include:

  • Name and business name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Website URL and industry details
  • Details about your business, workflows, and project needs
  • Any files or content you send us for a project

We may also collect limited technical information automatically when you visit our website, such as browser type, device type, and general usage patterns, through standard website analytics.

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide quotes or free audits
  • To deliver contracted websites, AI automation, marketing, or related services
  • To communicate about ongoing or prospective projects
  • To improve our website and service offerings
  • To meet legal, accounting, and contractual obligations

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for purposes beyond what is described here without your consent.

How information is shared

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With service providers who help us operate our business (for example, hosting, form-handling, and email-routing providers), who are only permitted to use the information to provide that service to us
  • Where required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your consent

Data storage and security

Information submitted through our website is processed and stored using reputable third-party infrastructure providers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; doing so may affect some site functionality.

Your rights

Under PIPA and PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for our use of your information, subject to legal or contractual restrictions
  • Request that we delete personal information we no longer need for the purposes it was collected

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes it was collected for, including any legal, accounting, or contractual requirements, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
